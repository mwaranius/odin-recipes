Lasagna

Ingredients

  • A willing cat
  • Orange spray paint
  • Black spray paint
  • Pre-made frozen lasagna

Steps

  1. Locate a cat and place it in the bathroom.
  2. Follow the directions on the box of frozen lasagna.
  3. Spray paint the cat orange.
  4. Spray paint black stripes on the cat to match the above photo.
  5. Place cat next to cooked lasagna.
  • MAY NOT WORK ON MONDAYS
