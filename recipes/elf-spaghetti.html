Elf Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Chocolate pop tarts
  • M&Ms
  • Mimi marshmallows
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Add spaghetti to the boiling water. Cook for 7-9 minutes.
  3. Place a large serving of spaghetti on a plate.
  4. Break up chocolate pop tarts by hand and sprinkle on top of the spaghetti.
  5. Add a large handful of M&Ms.
  6. Add a large handful of small marshmallows.
  7. Drench in Maple syrup.
  • KEEP A CONTAINER OF MAPLE SYRUP IN YOUR POCKET AT ALL TIMES
