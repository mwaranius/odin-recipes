Twinkie Wiener Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Twinkie
  • Hotdog
  • Easy Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut a twinkie in down the middle length-wise. Do not cut all the way through.
  2. Heat hotdog in boiling water or microwave.
  3. Place hotdog in the cut open twinkie.
  4. Spray hotdog with Easy Cheese.
  • CAN BE DIPPED IN COFFEE
